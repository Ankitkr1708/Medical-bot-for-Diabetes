Non - Diebetes

1. HbA1c (Hemoglobin A1c) Test
This test measures your average blood sugar level over the past 2 to 3 months.
Normal (Non-Diabetic): Below 5.7%
Pre-diabetes: 5.7% to 6.4%
Diabetes: 6.5% or higher

2. Fasting Blood Glucose (FBG) Test
This test measures your blood sugar after you have not eaten for at least 8 hours.
Normal (Non-Diabetic): Less than 100 mg/dL
Pre-diabetes: 100 to 125 mg/dL
Diabetes: 126 mg/dL or higher

3. Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT)
This test measures your blood sugar before and 2 hours after you drink a special sweet drink. It shows how your body processes glucose.
Normal (Non-Diabetic): Less than 140 mg/dL (after 2 hours)
Pre-diabetes: 140 to 199 mg/dL (after 2 hours)
Diabetes: 200 mg/dL or higher (after 2 hours)
